How hotBerry works
For more comfort and savings than ever, replace your existing thermostats controlling the boiler for hotBerry control. Access your boiler from anywhere, at any time via hotBerry web app on your phone. Easily program your boiler to have hot water by the time you get home or as you need.
Online Boiler Control
Using hotBerry ONLINE, you can manage all your home boiler requirements and retain full control over scheduling. When you log on, you will see all the principal control functions on your dashboard. Reporting is included as standard, and you can review historical schedule by the user or by day of hotBerry usage.
Your hotBerry web app is the best place to set your schedules and edit your settings. You may wish to rename or manage your profile or add new users to manage your hotBerry, and these changes are available on your Smartphone, laptop and mobile phone.
